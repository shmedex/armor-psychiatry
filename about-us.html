Our Story

Founded in 2010 by Dr. Sarah Williamson, Armor Psychiatry began as a small practice with a big vision: to provide accessible, compassionate mental health care that empowers individuals to live fulfilling lives.

What started as a solo practice has grown into a multidisciplinary team of psychiatrists, therapists, and mental health professionals, all united by our commitment to patient-centered care.

Over the years, we've helped thousands of patients navigate their mental health journeys, always adapting our approach to incorporate the latest evidence-based treatments while maintaining the personal touch that defines our practice.
